BENEFITS TO REALTORS

Dear Realtor®,

We Loan Officers have different ways to work with our Realtor partners. My way is quite straight forward. I would like to help you sell more houses by not only offering your buyers additional forms of financing, but also helping YOU in your efforts to have more clients. I do this by providing solutions to the most common problems faced by Realtors: How To Get More Clients and How to Keep the Ones You Already Have. I call this a "Helping Hand."

Regarding the finance programs for your buyers, I can provide 100% financing to borrowers with credit scores as low as 620. I also offer several programs for buyers who are self-employed or who otherwise choose not to provide their tax returns. I also can provide several types of ARM programs, with initial payment rates starting at just 2.5%.

I know that you probably get contacted by plenty of mortgage brokers, all telling you the same story about great rates and service, but in reality, any broker who does not offer these programs would soon be out of business. My value to you and your client is that I will do the following:

• Pre-qualify all of your prospects.

• Do a needs analysis of your client’s situation.

• Go beyond the offering of not only the best rate available for your clients for their particular situation, but also, and most importantly, the financial benefits that with time will bring the much needed solutions to their problems, which after all were the reason of why they came to you in the first place.

• Get you an electronic pre-approval so you can write up your offers faster.

• Process all the lender’s conditions in a timely fashion.

• Copy you on all lender’s conditions and their resolution.

• Be in constant communication with you updating you on the loan progress.

• Minimize the up-front closing costs by avoiding points whenever possible.

• Attend settlement to provide assistance to your buyer during the signing of the loan and mortgage documents.

• And perhaps more importantly, I will be clear and straight forward when telling you about your client's situation.

When working with your prospects, as an added value to the ones above, I will set you up with the following marketing aids that will keep you in your clients’ mind after the close of escrow. You can see how these marketing tools work by clicking on the buttons below. If you have any questions about how I can help you get more clients, please give me a call at (714) 610-6809 or send me a quick message. Thank you.
